Ah, come in, come in. Pull up a stool.

This is the new Seamus blog — a place for the stories that don't fit inside a voice call. Here you'll find:

- **Recipes** worth slowing down for
- **Technique deep-dives** — knife work, sauces, fermentation, the lot
- **Kitchen science** explained without the jargon
- **Behind-the-scenes** notes on what we're building next

## Why a blog?

Because cooking is about more than instructions. It's about *why* a sauce splits, *where* a dish comes from, and *how* one small change — a pinch more salt, a longer rest — turns a good meal into a great one.

Voice is brilliant for the moment you're standing at the stove. But sometimes you want to read, save, and come back to something. That's what this is for.

## What's next
